Sunny side landing page

A stunning colorful landing page, build with mostly HTML and CSS.

Project overview

I got the idea for the project from frontend mentor. They have projects for free and you get a few photos how the site looks like in the computer and mobile version. Paid version includes a figma file, which I do not have.

It was definitely challenging to make it look as similar as possible with just a few images, but I enjoyed the process to the last detail.

Project is made with flexbox and grid. There is a little bit of JavaScript.
