Sunny side landing page

A stunning colorful landing page, build with mostly HTML and CSS.

I got the idea for the project from frontend mentor. They have projects for free and you get a few photos (computer and mobile version). Paid version includes a figma file, which i do not have.

It was definitely challenging to make it look as similar as possible with just a few images, but i enjoyed the poscess to the last detail.

Project is made with flexbox and grid. The is a little bit of JavaScript
